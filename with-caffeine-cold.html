Wake Up and Smell the Caffeine!

Here are our top cold drinks with caffeine:


1. Blonde Vanilla Latte
"Blonde" because it uses a lighter roast of coffee beans, which results in a smoother and less bitter taste compared to a regular latte.

2. Matcha Green Tea
Made from matcha green tea powder, steamed milk, results in a creamy, frothy, and flavorful drink that is enjoyed by many tea lovers around the world.

3. Brown Sugar Oat Milk Shake and espresso
The brown sugar adds a depth of flavor and sweetness that complements the earthy notes of the oat milk and the bitterness of the espresso.

4. Pink Drink
It is a sweet and creamy iced drink made with a blend of Strawberry Acai Refresher and coconut milk, along with a scoop of strawberries.

2. Iced Chai Tea Latte
Creamy and spiced tea-based drink made with black tea, cold milk, and a blend of spices including cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves.

3. Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew
Creamy and sweet coffee drink with a hint of vanilla flavor, served over ice.
