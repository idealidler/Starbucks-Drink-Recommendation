Starbucks Jargons

1. Affogato: - A shot of espresso poured over a scoop of ice cream or gelato.

2. Cappuccino: - Espresso with steamed milk and a layer of frothed milk on top. (Mostly Foam)

3. Caramel Macchiato: - Vanilla flavoured milk with espresso shot on the top along with Caramel Drizzle.

4. Frappuccino: - Blended ice with different flavours.

5. Latte: - Espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of frothed milk on top.

6. Mocha: - Espresso with steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and whipped cream.

7. Nitro Cold Brew: - Cold brew coffee infused with nitrogen for a creamy and smooth texture.

8. Pike Place Roast: - Starbucks' signature medium roast coffee.

9. Pumpkin Spice Latte: - Espresso with steamed milk, pumpkin spice syrup, and whipped cream.

10. Venti: - The largest size for Starbucks drinks, equivalent to 20 fluid ounces.
