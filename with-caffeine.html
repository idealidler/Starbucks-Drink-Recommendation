Wake Up and Smell the Caffeine!

Here are our top hot drinks with caffeine:


1. Cafe Americano
A shot of espresso with hot water, for when you need a little extra kick in your step.

2. Freshly brewed coffees
A classic choice for coffee lovers, available in a variety of roasts and flavors.

3. Mocha
Made by combining steamed milk with espresso and chocolate syrup, and is often topped with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

4. Hot Chocolate (comes without espresso shot)
Pour steamed milk on top of chocolate flavoured mocha.

2. Regular hot Teas
Creamy and spiced tea-based drink made with black tea, cold milk, and a blend of spices including cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves.
